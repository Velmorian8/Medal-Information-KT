Award system of honor, virtue, and service in the Order

1. Medal of St. Augustine's Wisdom

Purpose: Given to the Wisest and Greatest Leadership in the Templar Order.

How to Earn: Be wise and show excellent leadership. (Only 1 knight can hold this. Approved only by Grandmaster, Master, and Seneschal.)

2. Medal of Saint Bernard of Clairvaux

Purpose: Given for moral leadership.

How to Earn: Host devotionals, guide others, bring peace, and uphold high moral discipline.

3. Medal of St. George, Protector of the True Cross

Purpose: Awarded to knights who protect the Church from hackers, trolls, and evil.

How to Earn: Defend the Faith and remain very active daily.

4. Medal of the Iron Cross

Purpose: For unmatched loyalty and leadership.

How to Earn: Lead events, carry in masses, and guide others with care.

5. Medal of Domus Dei

Purpose: For maintaining RC servers at peace.

How to Earn: Keep order and peace among the servers.

6. Medal of Custos Veritatis

Purpose: For upholding truth and justice.

How to Earn: Report corruption, resolve conflicts fairly, and make quick, just decisions.

7. Medal of Hugh de Payens

Purpose: For excellent leadership and vision.

How to Earn: Lead missions or projects successfully.

8. Crux Aurea Medal

Purpose: For peaceful acts, diplomacy, or moral service.

How to Earn: Prevent conflict, serve peacefully, and act with honor.

9. Medal of Militum Fidei

Purpose: For loyal knights of the faith.

How to Earn: Show humility and loyalty to clergy, congregation, and guests.

10. Medal of Sacra Virtus

Purpose: For humility, discipline, and virtue.

How to Earn: Maintain a clean record for 20+ days, mentor others, and uphold moral guidance.

11. Honorable Service Medal

Purpose: For consistent and loyal service.

How to Earn: Be active and helpful for 15+ days.

12. Medal of Honor

Purpose: For consistent knights with over 10+ days of service and peacekeeping.

How to Earn: Remain active and preserve peace in the Order.

Medals and Honors compiled by Velmorian, Grandmaster of the Knights Templar
